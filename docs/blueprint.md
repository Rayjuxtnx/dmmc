# **App Name**: DMMC Connect

## Core Features:

- About Us: Display information about the church, its mission, and leadership.
- Events Calendar: Display a calendar of upcoming church events, including service times, bible study and special events, with the possibility to register.
- Resources (Books & Media): Provide access to books, articles, and other media created and curated by the church, filter by category. Search bar implementation.
- Global Outreach: Share information and stories about the church's global missionary work and partnerships.
- Testimonials: Share stories and testimonials from church members. User review system implementation.
- Connect: Provide a contact form and information for getting in touch with the church, ministry leaders and related contact informations.
- Donation page: Easy integration for donations online.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep blue (#3F51B5), symbolizing trust, stability, and spirituality.
- Background color: Very light blue (#E8EAF6), providing a clean and serene backdrop.
- Accent color: Warm yellow (#FFEB3B), used to highlight important elements and calls to action.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', serif, for headings to convey a sense of elegance.
- Body font: 'PT Sans', sans-serif, for body text to ensure readability.
- Use simple, modern icons that are easily recognizable and thematically appropriate for each section.
- Maintain a clean, grid-based layout for easy navigation and readability, with clear separation of content sections.
